  • TMB_TRIDENT started a business post 3 days ago  

    DAILY TIP 6 ****BLACK MARKET**** In the black market you can buy whatever you need. You can purchase narcotics, weapons, cars and planes. You can also sell your items if needed, but you won't get as much back as you paid for them in the first place, so make your decisions wisely. Narcotics are mainly used to make your guys happy. Your defensive units need alcohol and weed to be happy. Your operatives need alcohol and coke to be happy. Weapons are used for attacks and defense as well as maintaining happiness. You can purchase Glocks, Shotguns, even Uzis and AK-47s. You must have one gun per defensive and can sell all weapons that are in excess of your defensive units. The more expensive the weapon, the more efficient it is in dealing death. Cars are used for drive-by attacks and stealing other player's cars. The Chrysler 300 seats 5 people and the S-Class limo seats 10 people. Planes are used to travel to other cities and stealing other player's planes. If you have enough planes, you won't need to purchase plane tickets to travel to other cities. The Gulfstream jet can fly 40 people and the Boeing 737-400 can fly 300 people.

    DAILY TIP 8 ****COLLECTING**** Intro. Collecting or producing drugs/cash etc is a way of raising money for the family. To collect money you need to hire either Card Dealers, Whores, Hustlers or punks. To produce Drugs/Alcohol and cash you need to hire thugs, bootleggers or punks. For the purpose of this guide we are going to hire Hustlers, the added bonus in hiring Hustlers, is that you also get Hitmen, which are a stronger DU than hiring Thugs and Bodyguards. The below guide will get you the maximum return for a 10 day round 1. Go to Hire Page 2. Set your Hire Payout to 100% 3.Select Hitmen & Hustlers 4.Enter the number of turns you want use to hire (All of them to maximise) 5.Select Scout 6.Go to Black Market Page 7.Buy 1 AK47 for each DU you have. Repeat steps 1 to 7, every 12 hours until the 5 Day, 5 Hour Mark. After the 5 Day, 5 Hour Mark, you need to collect every 12 hours, until the rounds end. 8.Go To Black Market Page 9.Buy Drugs and Alcohol (1 Million each) 10.Go to Hire Page and set your Hire Payout to either 0% or 1% 11.Go to Collect Page 12.Put your maximum amount of turns and hit Collect. 13.Go to Black Market Page and replenish your drugs. 14.Depending on your family instructions, transfer your cash to your family banker or put money in your family bank, via the Bank Page Repeat steps 8 to 14 until the end of round

    DAILY TIP 6 ****BLACK MARKET**** In the black market you can buy whatever you need. You can purchase narcotics, weapons, cars and planes. You can also sell your items if needed, but you won't get as much back as you paid for them in the first place, so make your decisions wisely. Narcotics are mainly used to make your guys happy. Your defensive units need alcohol and weed to be happy. Your operatives need alcohol and coke to be happy. Weapons are used for attacks and defense as well as maintaining happiness. You can purchase Glocks, Shotguns, even Uzis and AK-47s. You must have one gun per defensive and can sell all weapons that are in excess of your defensive units. The more expensive the weapon, the more efficient it is in dealing death. Cars are used for drive-by attacks and stealing other player's cars. The Chrysler 300 seats 5 people and the S-Class limo seats 10 people. Planes are used to travel to other cities and stealing other player's planes. If you have enough planes, you won't need to purchase plane tickets to travel to other cities. The Gulfstream jet can fly 40 people and the Boeing 737-400 can fly 300 people.
