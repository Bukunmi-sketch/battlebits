Buying Turns with WARS

Leaderboard Segregation

Players who use WARS for purchasing turns are classified into a "Paid Leaderboard". Those who haven't used WARS remain in the "Free Leaderboard".

Marketing Strategy

- Deploy targeted marketing campaigns focusing on the gaming community and cryptocurrency enthusiasts.
- Engage influencers and leverage social media for promotions and to grow player base.

Monetization

**Free Version: Operates as per basic game mechanics,
with WARS rewards for top players.

**Premium Version: Enhanced turn regeneration, exclusive items,
and advanced gameplay dynamics.

Technical Requirements

- Robust backend to manage player data, game mechanics,
and ensure fair play.

- Integration of a secure system for WARS cryptocurrency transactions
and rewards distribution

Further Development

- Regular content updates: New units, territories, and events to keep
the player base engaged.
- Collaborations with other games or platforms for special events
and crossover rewards.
