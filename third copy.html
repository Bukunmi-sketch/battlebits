Gaming Mechanics

Develop a turn-based multiplayer game focusing on army building, resource
management, and strategic engagements, with integrated WARS
cryptocurrency incentives.

Initial Setup

New players receive 3000 turns and start with zero army.
Turn regeneration: 15 turns every 10 minutes with a cap of 1800 turns.
Game rounds last 3 days.

Army Composition

- Infantry (weakest unit)
- Tanks
- Ships
- Planes (strongest unit)

Defensive Structures

- Walls
- Land mines
- Sea mines
- Anti-air guns

Economy

- Units and resources can be purchased with in-game currency or game turns.
